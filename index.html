About Us




Biddy cropscience is a sole proprietorship (individual) trader of bio pesticides, plant growth hormones and bio fertilizers.

Established in year 2017, we biddy cropscience are an acclaimed organization engaged in trading of Bio-Pesticides, Bio-Fertilizers, Bio-Fugicides and plant growth Hormones.We have several strategic marketing tie-ups with number of leading indian companies.We comprise of a team of intellectual and brilliant personal who enable us to deliver these quality products to our customers on time.
